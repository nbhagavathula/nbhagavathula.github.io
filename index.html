Personal Statement

My experience as a teacher has given me the valuable insight that teaching is more than just mere classroom instruction. I take teaching as a source of pleasure as it keeps extending the horizon of my knowledge. A good teacher takes her job as a challenge and has to constantly innovate to better her methods. Over the years I have brought forth many innovative methods and have enjoyed watching my methods work. I’ve especially been striving in shaping students who later on become assets to the society.

Being a volunteer psychological counsellor I concentrate more on motivating the child towards learning the subject with interest and understand the concept thoroughly. I believe in imparting value education to students, and I trained myself to do the same via attending multiple seminars and workshops on value-education. Mathematics, the queen of sciences, teaches us values, life skills practical values, moral values, cultural values, disciplinary values etc. Through my mathematics lessons, the child will be moulded into a positive-minded, self-confident person with much-needed self-esteem. They will learn that Mathematics with its interrelated topics like Algebra, Arithmetic, Trigonometry, Statistics, Geometry, Calculus etc make our day to day life very easy.Having done courses in Adult Psychology and Child Psychology as part of the curriculum in my B.Ed, I have been trying to motivate the children to show interest in studies and take the tests without any fear.

My experience has taught me that the difference between an intelligent and a "below-average" student is just the amount of time spent on practising problems; the gap can be closed effectively by making Math, Physics and Chemistry problems enjoyable in solving, thus, not alienating students of any background or ability. I strive to bring out the inner talent every child has within, at the same time taking care that the child is not carried away by his/her own ability. I feel always "Down to Earth" mentality makes a person humble and a good citizen. All through my career, I've been regarded as a very innovative teacher - employing novel methods and kits which have fetched me awards.
